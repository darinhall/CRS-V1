    Overview

    The Creator Accessory Kit gives you the tools to capture impressive and steady video with your compatible Canon EOS camera1 that you can later view, edit and share. The lightweight Tripod Grip HG-100BTR helps you frame your subject and minimize shake if handheld or provide shake-free results when stationery on a table, counter, or other surface. A detachable Bluetooth enabled remote gives you creative control and allows you to operate the camera from a distance. To complement the rich video quality, you can get crisp audio using the bundled Canon Stereo Microphone DM-E100 indoors, and even outdoors with the included windscreen.

    Compact and Lightweight Tripod Grip

    The Tripod Grip HG-100TBR is designed to help you capture fun, picture-perfect selfies at high and low angles with ease, as well as provide a stable base for shake-free videos while used as a tripod or if handheld. Its lightweight and compact design makes it ideal to take along for just about any activity planned.

    Achieve Impressive Audio

    Capture crisp audio with the Stereo Microphone DM-E100 microphone. With a lightweight design for portability and a hot-shoe mount for secure attachment, youll achieve high-quality audio while capturing your content.

    Remote Control Shooting

    With the included Wireless Remote Control BR-E1, you can easily direct the action while your camera stays in the perfect shooting position allowing you to take a group shot with your friends, or record your next inspiring video from a distance of up to approximately 16 ft. in any direction.
